Detailed Description

Precomputed basis-function fields on Gauss points.

dgBasisField stores GaussField representations of all polynomial basis functions and their spatial derivatives associated with a single DG cell.

The basis functions are evaluated at Gauss points inside the cell and on its faces using geometric information provided by dgGeomMesh.

This class is typically used during DG residual assembly to evaluate volume and surface integrals efficiently without recomputing basis functions at runtime.
