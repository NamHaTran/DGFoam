Detailed Description

Conservative thermodynamics wrapper for DG solvers.

dgThermoConservative reconstructs primitive thermodynamic quantities at DG Gauss points from conservative variables:

\[ (\rho,\; \rho\mathbf{U},\; E) \;\longrightarrow\; (T,\; p,\; he,\; a) \]

The model operates in a fully cell-local and Gauss-point-resolved manner and provides the stored thermodynamic and transport quantities required by compressible DG formulations. Additional properties such as \(C_p\), \(C_v\), \(\gamma\), \(\kappa\), \(\mu\), and \(\mathrm{Pr}\) are intended to be evaluated on demand through the thermoLaw and transportLaw APIs instead of being stored as dgField data. The stored thermodynamic energy variable he may represent either sensible internal energy or sensible enthalpy, depending on the selected energy model.

Supported model structure:

  • Equation of state (eqnOfState)
  • Thermodynamic law (thermoLaw)
  • Transport law (transportLaw)
  • Energy formulation (energyInternal only)

The class is intended exclusively for conservative-variable DG formulations of compressible flow and does not support primitive or incompressible models.
