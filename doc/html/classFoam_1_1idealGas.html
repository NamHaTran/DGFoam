Detailed Description

Ideal-gas equation of state.

idealGas implements the classical ideal-gas equation of state:

\[ p = \rho R T \]

where:

  • \(p\) is the thermodynamic pressure
  • \(\rho\) is the density
  • \(T\) is the temperature
  • \(R\) is the specific gas constant

This EOS:

  • Assumes calorically or thermally perfect gas behaviour
  • Is compatible with perfect-gas and thermal-perfect-gas thermo laws
  • Provides all fundamental thermodynamic relations required by dgThermoConservative
Supported relations
  • \(\rho = \frac{p}{R T}\)
  • \(p = \rho R T\)
  • \(T = \frac{p}{\rho R}\)
