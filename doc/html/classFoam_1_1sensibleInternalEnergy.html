Detailed Description

Sensible internal-energy formulation \(e = C_v T\).

sensibleInternalEnergy implements an energy model in which the conserved thermodynamic variable is the sensible internal energy.

The internal energy is defined as:

\[ e(T) = C_v(T)\,T \]

Enthalpy is recovered consistently as:

\[ h(T) = e(T) + \frac{p}{\rho} \]

This model:

  • Evolves sensible internal energy only
  • Excludes chemical formation energy
  • Is suitable for compressible DG solvers
  • Is compatible with perfect-gas and thermal-perfect-gas thermodynamics
Usage
sensibleInternalEnergy is intended for conservative DG formulations where the energy equation is written in terms of internal energy.
