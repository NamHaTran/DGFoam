27Class
28 Foam::dgGeomFace
29
30Description
31 Geometric representation of a DG face.
32
33 This class stores geometric and topological information associated
34 with a mesh face for Discontinuous Galerkin (DG) discretization,
35 including face orientation, reference-face mapping, Gauss-point
36 connectivity, and basis-function data on both owner and neighbour
37 sides.
