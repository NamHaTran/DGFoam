27Class
28 Foam::transportLaw
29
30Description
31 Transport laws define the molecular transport properties used in the
32 DG thermophysical system. They provide consistent evaluation of dynamic
33 viscosity μ(T,ρ), thermal conductivity κ(T,ρ), Prandtl number, and
34 related transport quantities at Gauss points.
35
36 Transport models are grouped into the following categories:
37
38 1. Constant-transport models
39 - μ, κ, and Pr are constant.
40 - No dependence on temperature or density.
41 - Useful for verification cases, laminar flows, and GPU-optimized
42 simulations.
43 - Compatible with all thermo and EOS models.
44
45 2. Sutherland-type ideal-gas transport
46 - Viscosity follows Sutherland's law:
47 μ = μ₀ (T/T₀)^(3/2) (T₀ + S) / (T + S).
48 - Thermal conductivity is computed via μ Cp / Pr.
49 - Valid for ideal gases at moderate temperatures.
50 - Compatible with perfect-gas thermo and ideal-gas or
51 thermal-perfect-gas EOS.
52
53 3. Polynomial / power-law transport
54 - Transport properties vary with temperature using empirical
55 correlations, e.g.:
56 μ(T) = a₀ + a₁ T + a₂ T² + ...,
57 μ(T) = a T^b.
58 - Suitable for high-temperature gases, combustion, and fluids
59 where viscosity changes strongly with temperature.
60 - Works with perfect-gas, constant-CpCv, absolute-enthalpy,
61 and selected real-gas thermo models.
62
63 4. Real-gas transport
64 - μ and κ depend on both temperature and density:
65 μ = μ(T, ρ), κ = κ(T, ρ).
66 - Required for supercritical fluids, hydrocarbons, cryogenic
67 fluids, and high-pressure real gases.
68 - Must be used together with real-gas EOS models
69 (Peng–Robinson, SRK, RK, etc.).
70
71 5. Kinetic / rarefied-gas transport (VHS/VSS/BGK/ES-BGK)
72 - Based on kinetic theory of gases.
73 - Viscosity and thermal conductivity follow power-law relations:
74 μ ∝ T^ω,
75 κ = μ Cp / Pr.
76 - Includes molecular parameters (collision diameter, molecular
77 mass, Boltzmann constant).
78 - Required for rarefied-gas flows, high-altitude aerothermodynamics,
79 and BGK-type models.
80 - Compatible with kinetic thermo and thermal-perfect-gas EOS.
