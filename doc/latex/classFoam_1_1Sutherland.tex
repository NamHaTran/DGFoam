\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Classical Sutherland-\/law viscosity model. 

Implements the classical \doxylink{classFoam_1_1Sutherland}{Sutherland} law for temperature-\/dependent dynamic viscosity\+:

\[
  \mu(T) = A_s \frac{T^{3/2}}{T + S}
\]

The Prandtl number is assumed constant\+:  \[
  \mathrm{Pr}(T) = \mathrm{Pr}_0
\]

Thermal conductivity is evaluated as\+:  \[
  \kappa(T) = \frac{\mu(T)\,C_p(T)}{\mathrm{Pr}_0}
\]

All evaluations are performed on per-\/cell \doxylink{classFoam_1_1GaussField}{Gauss\+Field} data.
