\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Precomputed basis-\/function fields on Gauss points. 

\doxylink{classFoam_1_1dgBasisField}{dg\+Basis\+Field} stores \doxylink{classFoam_1_1GaussField}{Gauss\+Field} representations of all polynomial basis functions and their spatial derivatives associated with a single DG cell.

The basis functions are evaluated at Gauss points inside the cell and on its faces using geometric information provided by \doxylink{classFoam_1_1dgGeomMesh}{dg\+Geom\+Mesh}.

This class is typically used during DG residual assembly to evaluate volume and surface integrals efficiently without recomputing basis functions at runtime.
