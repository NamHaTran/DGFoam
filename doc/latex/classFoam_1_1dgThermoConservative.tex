\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Conservative thermodynamics wrapper for DG solvers. 

\doxylink{classFoam_1_1dgThermoConservative}{dg\+Thermo\+Conservative} reconstructs primitive thermodynamic quantities at DG Gauss points from conservative variables\+:

\[
  (\rho,\; \rho\mathbf{U},\; E)
\;\longrightarrow\;
(T,\; p,\; he,\; a)
\]

The model operates in a fully cell-\/local and Gauss-\/point-\/resolved manner and provides the stored thermodynamic and transport quantities required by compressible DG formulations. Additional properties such as $C_p$, $C_v$, $\gamma$, $\kappa$, $\mu$, and $\mathrm{Pr}$ are intended to be evaluated on demand through the \doxylink{classFoam_1_1thermoLaw}{thermo\+Law} and \doxylink{classFoam_1_1transportLaw}{transport\+Law} APIs instead of being stored as \doxylink{classFoam_1_1dgField}{dg\+Field} data. The stored thermodynamic energy variable {\ttfamily he} may represent either sensible internal energy or sensible enthalpy, depending on the selected energy model.

Supported model structure\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Equation of state (\doxylink{classFoam_1_1eqnOfState}{eqn\+Of\+State})
\item Thermodynamic law (\doxylink{classFoam_1_1thermoLaw}{thermo\+Law})
\item Transport law (\doxylink{classFoam_1_1transportLaw}{transport\+Law})
\item Energy formulation (energy\+Internal only)
\end{DoxyItemize}

The class is intended exclusively for conservative-\/variable DG formulations of compressible flow and does not support primitive or incompressible models.
