\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Base class for energy models in DGFoam. 

Energy models define which thermodynamic energy variable is evolved by the DG solver and provide consistent conversions between temperature and energy representations.

Two categories of energy formulations are supported\+:


\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Internal-\/energy based models
\end{DoxyEnumerate}
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item The conserved variable is the sensible internal energy e(\+T).
\item Temperature is obtained from e(\+T) using \doxylink{classFoam_1_1thermoLaw}{thermo\+Law} relations.
\item Enthalpy is computed as\+: h = e + p/rho.
\item No formation enthalpy is included.
\item Suitable for compressible flows, shock-\/capturing, DG solvers, and real-\/gas thermodynamics.
\end{DoxyItemize}
\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Enthalpy-\/based models
\end{DoxyEnumerate}
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item The conserved variable is the sensible enthalpy h(\+T).
\item Temperature is obtained from h(\+T).
\item Internal energy is computed as\+: e = h -\/ p/rho.
\item Required when transport or chemistry formulations are expressed naturally in terms of enthalpy.
\end{DoxyItemize}

This base class provides a common interface for all energy models and supports runtime selection via dictionaries.
