\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Ideal-\/gas equation of state. 

\doxylink{classFoam_1_1idealGas}{ideal\+Gas} implements the classical ideal-\/gas equation of state\+:

\[
  p = \rho R T
\]

where\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item $p$ is the thermodynamic pressure
\item $\rho$ is the density
\item $T$ is the temperature
\item $R$ is the specific gas constant
\end{DoxyItemize}

This EOS\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Assumes calorically or thermally perfect gas behaviour
\item Is compatible with perfect-\/gas and thermal-\/perfect-\/gas thermo laws
\item Provides all fundamental thermodynamic relations required by dg\+Thermo\+Conservative
\end{DoxyItemize}

\begin{DoxyParagraph}{Supported relations}
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item $\rho = \frac{p}{R T}$ \item $p = \rho R T$ \item $T = \frac{p}{\rho R}$ \end{DoxyItemize}

\end{DoxyParagraph}
