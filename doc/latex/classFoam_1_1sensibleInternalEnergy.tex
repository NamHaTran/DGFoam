\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Sensible internal-\/energy formulation $e = C_v T$. 

\doxylink{classFoam_1_1sensibleInternalEnergy}{sensible\+Internal\+Energy} implements an energy model in which the conserved thermodynamic variable is the sensible internal energy.

The internal energy is defined as\+:  \[
  e(T) = C_v(T)\,T
\]

Enthalpy is recovered consistently as\+:  \[
  h(T) = e(T) + \frac{p}{\rho}
\]

This model\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Evolves sensible internal energy only
\item Excludes chemical formation energy
\item Is suitable for compressible DG solvers
\item Is compatible with perfect-\/gas and thermal-\/perfect-\/gas thermodynamics
\end{DoxyItemize}

\begin{DoxyParagraph}{Usage}
\doxylink{classFoam_1_1sensibleInternalEnergy}{sensible\+Internal\+Energy} is intended for conservative DG formulations where the energy equation is written in terms of internal energy. 
\end{DoxyParagraph}
